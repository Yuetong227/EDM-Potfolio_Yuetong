Here are the variables that we think may relate to the graduation rate and underpriveleged:

C150_4: completion rates for first-time, full-time students who begin school in the fall semester and complete within 150 percent of the expected time to completion

INEXPFTE: Instructional expenditures per FTE student

AVGFACSAL: average faculty salary

COSTT4_A: The average annual cost of attendance for academic year institutions

RET_FT4: retention rate of full-time students in the prior year, at four-year institution

INC_PCT_LO: the percentage of undergraduate students whose family incomes range is relatively low (0-30,000 dollars, which is the lowest range)
